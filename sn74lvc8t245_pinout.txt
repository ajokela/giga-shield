Key differences:
- SN74LVC8T245 has 24 pins (4 more: extra GND, extra VCC, DIR, split VCCA)
- DIR pin added (pin 1) - this is the key feature
- OE is active LOW on SN74LVC8T245 (vs active HIGH on TXB0108)
- A5-A8 shifted by one position due to extra GND at pin 6
- B-side pins similar but shifted
- Dual VCCA pins (22,23) and dual VCCB pins (17,24)
